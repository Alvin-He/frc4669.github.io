FIRST Robotics Competition 2020: Infinite Recharge

The Game

Two competing alliances consisting of three teams each race against the clock to score as many points as possible. After a short autonomous period at the beginning of the game, the human drivers take control of the robot. The game consists of shooting round "power cells" (foam balls) into target areas and spinning a control panel to the correct orientation once enough power cells have been shot into the targets. At the end of a match, robots can climb a hanging bar and attempt to balance with other robots to gain more points. The alliance scoring the most points wins the match.

Our Performance

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, all FRC competitions past week 2 were cancelled. This included the San Francisco and Silicon Valley Regionals, the two competitions we usually compete in. Thus, our team was unable to compete this year.
